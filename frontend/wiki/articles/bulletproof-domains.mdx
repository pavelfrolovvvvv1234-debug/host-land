- Works for marketing, fintech, VPN, media and privacy projects

### Step 4: Monitor Abuse Inbox

Keep abuse contacts active so we can coordinate remediation without escalation.

Bulletproof domains use offshore registrars with manual abuse handling that keep DNS online during disputes, unlike regular registrars that suspend automatically.

**Is WHOIS privacy included?**

Yes, WHOIS privacy is enabled by default at no extra cost for all bulletproof domains.

**What happens if someone files a complaint?**
