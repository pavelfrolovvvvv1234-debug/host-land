# Bulletproof Virtual Servers

Bulletproof VDS (Virtual Dedicated Server) hosting is a specialized service that keeps your virtual servers online even when complaints, DMCA notices, or abuse reports are filed against your content. This is achieved through offshore-friendly networks in the USA, Netherlands, Germany, and the wider EU.

## What is Bulletproof VDS?

Bulletproof VDS nodes prioritize uptime, privacy, and flexible acceptable use so growth campaigns, privacy tools, and security labs survive aggressive takedown attempts.

### Key Features

- **DMCA ignored by default** with manual review and mediation
- **Offshore routing** in NL, DE, MD and USA-friendly peers with 150+ Gbps capacity
- **Crypto, wire and anonymous support channels** without forced KYC
- Full root access for custom stacks, containers and tunnelling
- Snapshot, reinstall and rescue modes via panel or API
- Upgrade paths into Turbo VDS or dedicated nodes without downtime
